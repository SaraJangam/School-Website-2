CAMPUS LIFE


SCHOOL LIBRARY

School Library is the favourite place of the school

This is the great, and peaceful place where we can read thousands of books. The students can borrow the books, and after reading it they can return the books. The students get about 30 minutes to read the books. By reading the books they can make their vocab more stronger. Also, everyone in this world should get books to read. There are some children who don't get to read. So any book of your choice which you want to give or which you are done reading, you can donate those books.





SCIENCE LABROTARY

Science lab is experiment lab

In the science lab only 6th or 6th standard above students are allowed because it is dangerous. In the lab we make a lot of experiments. It is superb to do something different. Science laboratory is central to scientific instructions and it forms essential component of science education. It is in this place that various kinds of practical works are carry out by the students. In the lab teacher teaches the students very nicely. The teacher first shows the experiment and helps the students to the practical. The teachers and everyone in the school are kind and amazing.





COMPUTER LAB

Computer lab is a fun practical lab

In the computer lab we have a lot of fun. We make different projects. It is amazing. The teachers are also amazing they teach the students so nicely. The students have a lot of fun there, but they also have to follow the rules. In the computer lab the students learn amazing stuff. The teacher shows the screen with the help of projector, and then the students are able to see what teacher is doing. The students never get tired of learning. It is awesome.





PLAYGROUND

Playground has basketball and etc.

In the playgeround there is basketball net, archery and etc. You can also skate, do skipping too. It is really amazing. In the school races are organised too. There is garden near the playground. Our schools gardeners keep it clean all the time. It has nice grass, slides, swings and X & O board. The students have a lot of fun. The P.T teacher teaches the students skating and archery. The students get one hour to play.
